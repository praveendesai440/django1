K.L.E Technological University

(Vidya nagar, Hubli)

School of Computer Science and Engineering (SoCSE) offers graduate, post graduate and doctoral degree programs. The school provides a student centric learning environment with well equipped lab spaces, studio mode classrooms and smart classrooms. The vibrant learning environment is supported by large gathering areas for open houses, department seminars and discussion forums. The school is making multiyear efforts significantly expanding and building collaborations with industry and academic institutions for teaching learning and research. The curriculum is well designed by Board of Studies (BoS) constituted of experts from academia and industry. The curriculum encompasses core computer science courses and facilitates experiential learning. Few courses are co-designed and co-delivered by experts from the industry. The curriculum structure provides opportunity for students to enhance the research skills by providing research based REU/IRP courses and Minor program for interdisciplinary learning. The structure also gives space for developing entrepreneurial skills, by offering projects at Center for Technology Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CTIE).

The school has well experienced faculty with expertise in the areas of Data Engineering, System Engineering and Network Engineering. SoCSE is a hub of activities in the areas Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Analytics, High Performance Computing (HPC) and Cloud Computing and Networking. To enhance the research eco system, state of the art research infrastructure in the above areas has been setup. Co-curricular and extracurricular activities are conducted by the school to enhance interpersonal and leadership qualities. Students have participated in various national and state level events including KPIT Sparkle, Bosch INSCRIBE, Accenture Innovation Jockeys, and ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) for successive years and have won many accolades. SoCSE has been regularly hosting hackathons including ‘Smart India Hackathon’ of MHRD and AICTE since three years. Apart from this hackathons from industries like Samsung R&D, Juniper Network and INSZoom are conducted regaularly. This has enabled students to excel in various national and international level competitions. This has also resulted in students getting high package offers. The school has produced quality professionals who are holding responsible positions and have added value to the IT industry across the globe. The school has good placement record with offers from companies like Microsoft, Samsung, Walmart, Juniper, AWS, Akamai, SAP, Sony, Informatica, etc..
